FlowState Privacy Policy

Effective July 15, 2026 · flowstatesurf.org · Contact: riversurforegon@gmail.com

The short version: FlowState is built by a river surfer, for river surfers. We collect the minimum needed to run the app — your email if you sign in, your location only at the moment you check in or upload, and anonymous usage taps. We never sell data, never run ads, and never share your information with anyone.

What we collect

  • Account info. If you sign in (by email or Google), we store your name and email address. Google sign-in shares only your basic profile (name, email, picture) — we never see your password or anything else in your Google account.
  • Check-ins. When you check in at a wave, we record the wave, the time, and your GPS position at that moment — this is how check-ins are verified as real. We do not track your location at any other time, and there is no background location access.
  • Flow Proof uploads. Photos/videos you upload are public to other FlowState users, stamped with the river flow and your surfer handle (the part of your email before the @). Your full email is never shown publicly.
  • Messages. Direct messages between members are stored so they can be delivered. They are not visible to other users or to any third party.
  • Usage data. Anonymous taps and screen views (e.g. "someone opened the map") that help improve the app. No advertising identifiers, no cross-site tracking, no cookies beyond what the app needs to function.

Where it lives

Data is stored with Supabase (our database provider) in the United States. River flow data comes from public government sources (USGS, Bureau of Reclamation, NOAA, Environment Canada) and involves no personal data.

What we never do

  • Sell, rent, or share your personal data with anyone.
  • Show ads or use advertising trackers.
  • Track your location outside of the check-in or upload you explicitly initiate.

Your choices

  • Use the app without signing in — browsing flows requires no account.
  • Deny location access — the app still works; only check-ins and uploads need GPS.
  • Delete your data — email riversurforegon@gmail.com and we will remove your account data, check-ins, messages, and uploads.

Changes

If this policy changes, the effective date above changes with it. Material changes will be noted in the app.
